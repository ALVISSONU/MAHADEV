MAHADEV

May all your wishes come true & the blessing of Shiva remain with you always

May the divine glory remind you of your capabilities, and help you in attaining success

Alertness, Knowledge and wisdom are all related to third eye

Lord Shiva is considered the father of all sounds, languages, music and vibrations in the universe.

Wisdom is beyond the mind, but it needs to be expressed with a tinge of mind and this is symbolised by the crescent moon, which adorns the forehead of lord shiva.

Vahanas symbolize or complement the energy or character of their deity. The white color of the bull symbolizes purity and justice.
